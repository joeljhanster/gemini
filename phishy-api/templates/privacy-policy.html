Phishy Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Phishy, a Chrome extension developed by MrHanTheMan ("we" or "us"), collects, uses, and stores your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

Phishy collects the following information from your browsing activity:

  • URLs:

    • When you visit a webpage, Phishy analyzes the URLs of links present on that page. We do not collect the content of the webpages themselves.

Use of Information

We use the collected URLs for the following purposes:

  • Phishing Detection:

    • We send the URLs to our secure API powered by Gemini, an AI model trained to detect phishing attempts. Gemini analyzes the characteristics of the URLs to determine if they are suspicious. We do not use the URLs for any other purpose besides phishing detection.

  • Analysis and Improvement (Optional):

    • We may use anonymized data (without any user identification) to analyze phishing trends and improve the accuracy of our detection system. This helps us better protect users from future phishing attempts.

Data Storage

We take the security and privacy of your information seriously. Here's how we store the collected data:

  • URLs:

    • The URLs you visit are temporarily stored while being analyzed by Gemini. We do not store them long-term and they are not associated with any user identifier.

  • API Response:

    • The response received from Gemini regarding the suspiciousness of a URL might be stored for a limited period for analysis purposes. This data is anonymized and does not contain any user identification.

User Control

Currently, Phishy does not offer options for users to directly manage the data collected. However, you can always uninstall the extension if you are uncomfortable with the data collection practices.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use HTTPS to encrypt communication between your browser and our API.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at gemini.phishy@gmail.com.
